= Preface

This manual, a comprehensive document issued by the Standards Group & Unified Material Code Cell of Corporate R&D at Bharat Heavy Electricals Limited, Hyderabad, serves as a detailed guide to the Unified Material Codification (UMCD) system. The UMCD system is a testament to BHEL’s commitment to standardization, efficiency, and innovation in material management.

The manual delves into the intricacies of the 12-digit material codification scheme, which has been meticulously designed to streamline the identification, classification, and digitization of materials across the corporation. 
It reflects the collaborative efforts of various inter-plant committees aiming to unify material codes and promote a systematic and synergistic approach to inventory control.

As you navigate through the pages, you will encounter the historical background of material codification at BHEL, the objectives of the UMCD system, and the significant features that make this scheme a cornerstone of BHEL’s materials management strategy. 
The manual also provides insights into the three-tier system, error prevention mechanisms, and the importance of adhering to the codification standards.

It is our hope that this manual will not only enhance the understanding of the UMCD system but also serve as a valuable reference for all stakeholders involved in the procurement, storage, and management of materials at BHEL.

(V V Subrahmanyam)

GM & Head (Corporate Research & Development)
