= Standardization Objectives

* Strengthen standardization to promote optimum resource utilization.
* Reduce variety and establish unified technological base for the company.
* Implement and tap technological gains to the company, the customers and to the country at large.
* Promote cause of standardization and spread the philosophy, interacting with the national, international and related standardization agencies.
* Standardize at product, plant and corporate levels. 
Rationalize existing practices, unify plant codes.
* Use standardization as a management tool to monitor and elevate engineering and production culture of the company.
